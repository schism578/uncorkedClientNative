Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a wine?
After logging in, tap the Home tab and fill in the wine details. You can attach a photo from your camera or library before saving.

How do food pairings work?
Open any wine from your collection and tap "Pair It." uncorked will suggest complementary foods based on the wine's profile. You can also record your own pairings manually.

Can I edit a wine or pairing after saving?
Yes — tap any entry in your history to expand it, then use the Edit button to update any field.

How do I delete a wine?
Open the wine's detail card in your history and tap Delete.

Is my data private?
Yes. Your collection is stored securely under your account and is never shared with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for details.
