Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 1, 2026

uncorked ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding it.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account and use uncorked, we collect:

  • Account information: your email address and a securely hashed password.
  • Wine and pairing data: the wine entries, food pairings, tasting notes, ratings, and photos you choose to save. This data belongs to you.

We do not collect device identifiers, location data, or usage analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to operate the app:

  • To authenticate your account and store your wine collection securely.
  • To generate food pairing suggestions based on the wine details you provide. Suggestions are generated using an AI service; your data is not retained by that service beyond the duration of a single request.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on a secured cloud server. We use industry-standard practices including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and hashed passwords. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect your information.

4. Photos

Photos you attach to wine or pairing entries are stored as part of your account data on our servers. We do not access, analyze, or share these photos. You can remove a photo at any time by editing the entry.

5. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at uncorkedappmedia@gmail.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

uncorked is not directed at children under 17. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of 17. Because the app relates to alcoholic beverages, it is intended for adults of legal drinking age in their jurisdiction.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at:
uncorkedappmedia@gmail.com
